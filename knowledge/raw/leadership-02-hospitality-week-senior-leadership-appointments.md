# Chipotle makes new appointments to senior leadership team

By Cynera Rodricks — Monday, 26 January 2026

Chipotle Mexican Grill has announced new hires to its senior leadership team appointing Ilene Eskenazi as chief legal and human resources officer following the departure of Roger Theodoredis.

Theodoredis has transitioned out of his role as chief legal officer and general counsel. Eskenazi joined Chipotle in 2023 as chief human resources officer. Her professional background includes senior legal and human resources roles at Petco Health and Wellness Company, Boardriders, and Red Bull North America.

In a further change to the executive team, vice president of brand marketing Stephanie Perdue will serve as interim chief marketing officer.

She succeeds Chris Brandt, who has left his position as president and chief brand officer.

The company has also commenced a search for a permanent marketing chief.

Both Theodoredis and Brandt will remain with the business in advisory roles for a limited period to assist with the transition.

Additionally, the company reaffirmed its full-year 2025 financial guidance and stated that its 2026 strategic plan remains unchanged.

Full fiscal year results are scheduled for release on 3 February 2026.

Scott Boatwright, chief executive of Chipotle, said: "Ilene is a valued member of our executive leadership team, and her perspective has been instrumental over the years.

"Chipotle has a deep bench of marketing talent, and we are fortunate to have Stephanie take on the interim role overseeing our strategic marketing initiatives, customer engagement and incredible brand loyalty. I would like to thank Roger and Chris for their leadership and many contributions throughout their time at Chipotle."
