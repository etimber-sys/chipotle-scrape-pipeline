# Chipotle Mexican Grill — Management Team

## Scott Boatwright — Chief Executive Officer

Scott Boatwright is the Chief Executive Officer and a member of the Board of Directors for Chipotle Mexican Grill. Appointed in November 2024, Mr. Boatwright previously served as Interim Chief Executive Officer since August 2024. Prior to that, he held the role of Chief Operating Officer/Chief Restaurant Officer since May 2017. Mr. Boatwright was named "Operations CREATOR of the Year" by Nation's Restaurant News in 2021 and named to Fast Casual's "2020 Top 25 Executives" list.

Prior to Chipotle, Mr. Boatwright spent 18 years with Arby's Restaurant Group in various leadership positions. He most recently served as the Senior Vice President of Operations, where he was responsible for the performance of over 1,700 Arby's restaurants in numerous states.

Mr. Boatwright is a member of Business Roundtable, Business Council, and National Restaurant Association's Fast Casual Industry Council. He holds a Master of Business Administration from the J. Mack Robinson College of Business at Georgia State University.

---

## Adam Rymer — Chief Financial Officer

Adam Rymer is the Chief Financial Officer for Chipotle Mexican Grill, responsible for corporate and field financial planning and analysis, as well as the financial reporting, tax, treasury and investor relations functions. He previously served as Vice President of Finance and has held a variety of positions in Finance and People Experience since joining the organization in 2009. In 2025, Mr. Rymer was named Extel All America Best CFO for the restaurant sector.

Prior to Chipotle, Mr. Rymer held corporate finance positions at Rock Bottom Restaurants and Travelocity and was also a Which Wich franchisee.

Mr. Rymer has a Bachelor of Business Administration degree in finance from the University of Texas at Austin.

---

## Curt Garner — President, Chief Strategy and Technology Officer

Curt Garner is the President, Chief Strategy and Technology Officer for Chipotle Mexican Grill. Prior to this, he served as the Chief Customer and Technology Officer for nearly a decade since joining the organization in November 2015. In 2020, Mr. Garner was named to the CIO Hall of Fame, and in 2025, he was featured on Forbes's CIO Next list.

Prior to Chipotle, Mr. Garner worked at Starbucks Corporation for 17 years, most recently serving as Executive Vice President and Chief Technology Officer. He led the brand's global technology and engineering services and was responsible for creating and building the Starbucks app, loyalty program, website and mobile payment.

Mr. Garner has a Bachelor of Arts degree in economics from The Ohio State University.

---

## Laurie Schalow — Chief Corporate Affairs and Food Safety Officer

Laurie Schalow is the Chief Corporate Affairs and Food Safety Officer for Chipotle Mexican Grill. She oversees all internal and external communications, stakeholder engagement, sustainability, customer service, social listening, government affairs, food safety and quality assurance for the brand's 4,000+ restaurants. She also serves as President of The Chipotle Cultivate Foundation.

Prior to joining Chipotle in 2017, Ms. Schalow was Vice President of Public Affairs for Yum! Brands, overseeing global corporate social responsibility, public relations, crisis management, social listening and community diversity programs for KFC, Pizza Hut and Taco Bell restaurants in 140 countries.

Ms. Schalow holds a Bachelor of Science degree in business from Miami University and an MBA from Case Western Reserve and Wayne State University.

## Ilene Eskenazi — Chief Legal and Human Resources Officer

Ilene Eskenazi is the Chief Legal and Human Resources Officer for Chipotle Mexican Grill. Appointed in January 2026, Ms. Eskenazi previously served as the Chief Human Resources Officer for two years at the organization.

Prior to joining Chipotle, Ms. Eskenazi served as the Chief Legal and Human Resources Officer for Petco Health and Wellness Company. Before that, she was the Global General Counsel and Chief Human Resources Officer at Boardriders, Inc. (previously Quiksilver, Inc.). She also held the post of Chief Legal Officer and Senior Vice President of Talent Operations and Performance at True Religion Apparel, Inc. as well as senior leadership positions at Red Bull North America, Inc. and The Wonderful Company.

Ms. Eskenazi serves on the Board of Directors of a.k.a. Brands Holding Corp. She holds a Juris Doctorate from UCLA School of Law and a Bachelor of Arts in philosophy from the University of Michigan.

## Jason Kidd — Chief Operating Officer

Jason Kidd is the Chief Operating Officer at Chipotle. He joined the organization in 2025 and is responsible for overseeing operations of the company's 4,000+ restaurants as well as development for its new restaurant openings.

Prior to Chipotle, Mr. Kidd served as the Global Chief Operating Officer at Taco Bell. Before joining Taco Bell, he held the role of President of Hearing Lab Technology, LLC. Mr. Kidd spent six years leading operations at 99 Cents Only Stores, most recently serving as President and Chief Operating Officer. Additionally, he held roles of increasing responsibility across operations, merchandising, planning and supply chain at Sam's Club for 20 years.

Mr. Kidd holds a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration from the Sam M. Walton College of Business at the University of Arkansas.

## Kerry Bridges — Chief Food Safety Officer

Kerry Bridges is the Chief Food Safety Officer for Chipotle Mexican Grill. Appointed in January 2026, Ms. Bridges previously served as Vice President of Food Safety.

Prior to joining Chipotle, Ms. Bridges oversaw supplier food safety for Wal-Mart. During her time at Wal-Mart, Kerry's food safety oversight included tens of thousands of Wal-Mart's, Neighborhood Market's, and Sam's Club's food suppliers. Prior to Wal-Mart, Ms. Bridges' career included roles with TESCO and Jack in the Box, Inc.

She attended California Polytechnic State University, where she received a bachelor's degree in food science.

## Stephanie Perdue — Interim Chief Marketing Officer

Stephanie Perdue is the Interim Chief Marketing Officer. Appointed in January 2026, Ms. Perdue previously served as Vice President of Brand Marketing since 2018, overseeing brand strategy, advertising, innovation, experiential, social media and brand activation.

Prior to Chipotle, Ms. Perdue served as the Chief Marketing Officer at TGI Fridays. She also held senior leadership roles at Taco Bell for over 12 years, ultimately serving as the Chief Product Marketing Officer, responsible for driving marketing strategy and innovation, including leading the teams that designed Doritos Locos Tacos, Taco Bell breakfast and the value menu.

Stephanie holds an MBA from the UCLA Anderson School of Management and a business economics undergraduate degree from UCLA.

## Nate Lawton — Chief Business Development Officer

Nate Lawton is the Chief Business Development Officer for Chipotle Mexican Grill, overseeing international business development opportunities, emerging concepts and partnership strategy.

Prior to joining Chipotle, Mr. Lawton spent over 20 years at Procter & Gamble working across Fabric Care, Beauty Care, Personal Health Care and Food & Beverage. He ultimately served as the Vice President of North America Fabric Care. Mr. Lawton also co-developed and sold his own business, George Remus Bourbon, to MGP Ingredients Inc. in 2016.

He holds a bachelor's degree in business administration from Emory University's Goizueta Business School.

## Nicole West — Vice President, Digital Experiences

Nicole West is the Vice President of Digital Experiences for Chipotle Mexican Grill. She is responsible for Chipotle.com, the Chipotle app, Chipotle Rewards and Chipotle Digital Kitchens. She oversees robotic innovation, UI/UX design, product management, and Chipotle's integrated 'customer-first' digital roadmap. Under Ms. West's leadership, Chipotle's digital business reached the $4 billion-dollar revenue milestone in 2024.

Ms. West holds a Bachelor of Science degree in geography and environmental studies and a Master of Public Administration and Environmental Policy degree from University of Colorado, Denver.

## Nevielle Panthaky — Vice President, Culinary

Nevielle Panthaky is the Vice President of Culinary at Chipotle Mexican Grill, overseeing culinary strategy, menu innovation, product commercialization and cross-functional collaboration. Chang's, Panda Restaurant Group and Dine Brands.

## Michael (MJ) Johnston — Vice President of Finance

Michael Johnston is the Vice President of Finance at Chipotle Mexican Grill, leading the company's global Finance organization. He oversees Corporate, Field, Development and Sales/Supply Chain Finance and has spent nearly 11 years with Chipotle.

## Garrett Calderwood — Vice President, Operations Services

Garrett Calderwood is the Vice President of Operations Services at Chipotle Mexican Grill, overseeing the strategies, tools and support that enable operational excellence across the company's restaurants. Mr. Calderwood joined Chipotle over 11 years ago, most recently serving as Regional Vice President responsible for over 450 restaurants across the Northeast U.S.
